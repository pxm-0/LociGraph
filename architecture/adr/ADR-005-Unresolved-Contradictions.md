# ADR-005: Contradictions Are Unresolved By Default

## Decision

Contradictions are not automatically resolved as conflict or evolution.

## Reason

Contradictions may be held differently by different users.

A contradiction can be:
- true conflict
- evolution
- contextual difference
- both simultaneously
- neither until examined

The system should not collapse ambiguity too early.

## Consequence

Contradictions default to unresolved.

The user, assisted by the Custodian, can classify them later.
