# ADR-004: Concepts Are Created, Not Discovered

## Decision

LociGraph treats concepts as created objects.

## Reason

Discovery requires a separate criterion:
what constitutes discovery rather than creation?

To avoid premature metaphysical commitments, the system creates concepts from evidence,
interpretation, clustering, naming, and user approval.

## Consequence

Concepts have provenance:
- creator: AI, user, or hybrid
- evidence
- confidence
- revision history
- merge history
