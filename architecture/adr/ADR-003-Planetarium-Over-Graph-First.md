# ADR-003: Planetarium Over Graph-First

## Decision

The primary high-level visualization is a planetarium, not a flat graph.

## Reason

Traditional graph views show nodes and edges but fail to express weight as a meaningful spatial variable.

The planetarium allows:
- mass
- orbit
- brightness
- rings
- gravity
- semantic distance
- conceptual motion

Also: it is cool.

## Consequence

Graph data remains part of the system, but the endgame navigation metaphor is spatial and celestial.
