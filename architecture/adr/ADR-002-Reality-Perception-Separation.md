# ADR-002: Reality and Perception Separation

## Decision

Reality and perception are modeled separately.

## Reason

The system must remain factual despite perception and interpretation differences.

A factual event and a user's interpretation of that event are not the same object.

## Example

Reality:
- A person stopped responding.

Perception:
- This felt like abandonment.

## Consequence

Custodian responses must distinguish:
- what happened
- what was felt
- what was inferred
- what was later believed
- what the model currently thinks
