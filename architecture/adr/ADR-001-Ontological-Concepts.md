# ADR-001: Ontological Concepts

## Decision

LociGraph is concept-centric. Concepts are treated as ontological objects inside the system.

## Reason

The system is not merely storing notes, messages, or source files. It is modeling broader structures:
systems theory, polymathic connections, recurring abstractions, and conceptual evolution.

## Consequence

The primary user workflow revolves around concepts, not documents.

Observations and claims exist to support, revise, or challenge concepts.
